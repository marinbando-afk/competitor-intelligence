How it actually works

It reads their real data — it doesn't make things up

"Isn't this just ChatGPT spitting out generic guesses?" No — and the difference is entirely in what the AI is allowed to look at.

1
Capture, every day. We pull each competitor's live ads, organic posts, email campaigns and a full snapshot of their storefront — a fresh, dated record. Their actual moves, not a model's memory.
2
Read it deeply. The AI watches each video ad and transcribes what's actually said, studies every creative, and reads the storefront — so it works off what's really there, including audio and on-screen text.
3
Compare to yesterday. It diffs today's capture against the last one and keeps only what genuinely changed — a new angle, a price drop to the cent, a new product, a sale starting.
4
Ground every line. Each takeaway is tied to the exact ad, price or post it came from, with a link to the source. If it can't point to it, it doesn't say it.
