FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly does it track?
Meta & TikTok ads, landing and product pages, pricing, discounts, bundles, offers, and email campaigns — for every competitor you add. One report covers all four channels.
How fast will I see changes?
Every competitor is scanned daily, and you get one consolidated briefing each morning. Big moves — a major discount or a new funnel — are flagged the day they happen.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Paste your competitors' URLs and you're done. No pixels, no code, no browser extensions — and nothing ever touches your own store.
Is this actually allowed?
Yes. We only monitor publicly available information — public ads in the Meta & TikTok ad libraries, public web pages, and the emails you'd receive as a normal subscriber. Nothing private.
Which competitors can I track?
Any eCommerce brand with a website and active ads. It works especially well for DTC brands on Shopify and anyone advertising on Meta or TikTok.
